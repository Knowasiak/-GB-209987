# GB-209987
A zombie game developed by school friends, Aditya Gaurav &amp; Rahul Pandit.

# Overview
GAJ8 zombies is based on an cloudy city featuring many types of enemies, defending methods, buildings to go into and explore find guns and weapons, all done into a drowning dusk
environment, with some living AI around and a beautiful place to roam and explore around! :D

 # Controls and Gameplay
Controls and the gameplay is very easy to use yet it is a fun challenge to kill zombies ahead of you and get the flag of triumph! 

Use the left side of your android mobile to control character movement, and left side to get the camera view angle. Guns can be found anywhere laying around the map on the ground or inside buildings or residences. Zombies have a good eye on you, so make sure you hide at a good place before you go in from of all of them.
Waves are counted as your score, that how many waves you survived and killed the scary zombies.

A special gun is spawned in the garden near the railway lines, which gives really high dmaage to boss zombies.
